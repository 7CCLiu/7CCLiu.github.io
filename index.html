About me

I am a Ph.D candidate at CSE of CUHK under supervision of Prof. John C.S. Lui. I received a B.S in statistics from USTC in 2022.

Currently, I am visiting CUHK (SZ), hosted by Prof. Zhi-Quan (Tom) Luo. I visited HKUST during the summer of 2021, hosted by Prof. Tong Zhang. I work closely with Prof. Luo Luo.

I am always open for possible collaborations on optimization and machine learning, please feel free to contact me.

Research

My research focuses on designing efficient methods for large-scale optimization problems. I am particularly interested in the following fields:

  1. The theory of second-order optimization.

  2. Distributed optimization.

Email me if you have any questions about my work.
