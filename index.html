About me

I am a Ph.D candidate at CSE Department of CUHK under supervision of Prof. John C.S. Lui. I received a B.S in statistics from USTC in 2022.

Previously, I visited CUHK (SZ) during the summer of 2024, hosted by Prof. Zhi-Quan (Tom) Luo. I visited HKUST during the summer of 2021, hosted by Prof. Tong Zhang. I work closely with Prof. Luo Luo.

I am always open for possible collaborations or visiting opportunities from both academia and industry on optimization and machine learning, please feel free to contact me.

Research

My research focuses on designing efficient methods for large-scale optimization problems. I am particularly interested in the following fields:

  • The theory of second-order optimization.

  • Distributed/Federated optimization.

Feel free to email me if you have any questions about my work or are interested in collaborations.
