About me

I received a B.S in statistics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2022.

I am interesed in optimization and machine learning.

Research

My recent research focus on designing computation (and communication) efficient second-order methods. I am also interested in stochastic optimization, federated learning and their applications in machine learning.

If you are interested in my works and want to discuss or collaborate with me, feel free to drop me an email.
