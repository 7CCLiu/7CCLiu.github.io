About me

I received a B.S in statistics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2022.

I am interesed in optimization and machine learning. I am always open for possible collaborations. Please feel free to contact me.

Research

My recent research focus on designing efficient methods (especially second-order methods) for large-scale optimization problems with strong theoretical guarantees.

Email me if you have any questions about my works.
