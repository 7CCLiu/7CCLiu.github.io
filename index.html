About me

I am a first year Ph.D student at the Chinese Unversity of Hong Kong (CUHK). I received a B.S in statistics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2022. I am interesed in optimization and machine learning.

Research

My current research is about second-order optimization and distributed optimization.

Previously, I was a summer intern in Prof. Tong Zhang's research group. Besides, I work closely with Prof. Luo Luo at Fudan University.
